Music

I've been playing the guitar for about 6 years now since I graduated high school. Though it's been a long time, I'd still classify myself as a beginner that practices often. As much as I like to listen to music, I like to learn how to play them on the guitar. I like to experiment and acquire new skills as I practice but I usually play fingerstyle guitar. Through the years, I've learned plenty of different songs through YouTube that range from lofi music to instrumental. One artist that I look at for inspiration when I play guitar is Keshi who is a indie-pop artist. I really like the style and chords that he uses in his songs so I try to learn how to play them, but I also want to create my own music. One day, I hope to not only play music but to write lyrics in order to create something that is my own.

I find music to be a creative outlet for self-expression and a way to self-soothe your mind. My taste in music is pretty diverse as I like hip-hop, indie, pop, rock and instrumental music. I'd say that, like some types of people I listen to certain genres depending on my mood. For instance, when I need to concentrate while studying I listen to instrumental music because it does not have lyrics that tend to distract me.

As I've said I like to listen to different types of music, and though I do enjoy playing the guitar I took interest in the piano. My grandpa plays the piano quite often and it's a popular instrument that is used. A couple of months ago, I had found a keyboard next to the trash so I decided I'd try to learn how to play it. I've only ever really heard it being played so it was difficult to know where to start. I began by watching paino tutorial videos on YouTube to learn the keys and basics of piano. Once I got familiar with the keys, I attempted to learn "River Flows in You" by Yiruma which is a very popular and difficult piece. As I am a beginner, I learned and practiced how to play the easier version. Although I am not perfect at the piano, I strive to practice more so I can learn to read piano sheet music as I play.
